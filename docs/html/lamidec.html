lamidec.plot_xdepth_rsa.plot_xdepth_rsa(results_path, dvar, thr, tw, norm, diff, dvar_diff, upsample)Â¶

Plot cross-depth RSA

This function plots the output performed by the function xdepth_rsa.py representational similarity analysis (RSA) between any two depth-derived representational similarity matrices (RDMs). First, stimulus-specific RDMs, originally computed for individual time points and trial halves (even/odd), are grouped within specified time windows. Subsequently, the Spearman correlation coefficient between all even/odd pairs of depth-dependent time-averaged RDMs is computed. Correlations between RDMs derived from different variable features are obtained and visualized as Depth x Depth correlation matrices/maps (e.g. with gamma-derived RDMs in one axis and theta-derived RDMs in the othr axis, either obtaind from spectral power or a phase measure such as the Goodnes of Phase Relation (GPR), or both measures combined). This approach allows the comparison of information content carried by different variable features, such as spectral estimates of power and phase at different frequency bands and laminar depths.
